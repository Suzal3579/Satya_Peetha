About us

 

The Gayatri Shakti Peetha started functioning in the year 1970 under Gita Prachar Vidya Nilaya Trust. The founder Trustee members Mr. Boragaela Venkata Rao and Mr Rama Yogeswar Rao have taken keen intrest for registering the Trust and aquiring the land for the Shakti Peetha. Guru Sri Ramananda Swami as a B Sc student of SKBR College, Aamalapuram was associated with sadhu SubbaRaju of Chintalapudi for practicing Yoga, Pranayama and spreading the Sanskrit literature among the people. Brahmachari Guruji Ramananda Swami was associated with the shram in 1973.He got dikshit as a Ramanandi (Rama Mantra)and was responsible for spreading spiritualism among the people in and around Amalapuram. A group of intellectual Brahmins(Vishalakshi maa) started constructing the Krishna Temple. Under the spiritual leadership of the Guru they used to practice Lalita Sahashra nama, Vishnu Sahashra Nama and Gita Parayan.

In 1999 the Datta traya temple was constructed in the auspicious presence of Bithala baba. Getting the instruction from Guru Pandit Shriram Sharma Acharya, Shantikunj, Haridwar (while meditation) the Guru established the Gayatri Temple and started performing Yagna and spreading it to other people. The Ashram started with a thatched house and regular Japa, meditation and Yagna was conducted. A number of devotees joined. Subhadra Raju from Rangapuram and others put their best effort to develop the Ashram. Two small rooms were constructed with the contribution of devotees and the causisn sister of the Guruji. A lot of Tapa, Yoga and Meditation was practiced. As per the saying of the Guru while constructing the 2nd room Yoga Sarpa(Five headed) with big Siva linga was seen (discovered) which was allowed to remain as it is inside.

Later on the new Trustee members were appointed to look after the Ashram. Mr. Arabinda Ghosh, Tatavarthi Govinda Rao, Siba, Dr Verma and Srinivas Rao were instrumental in managing the Ashram and the Saraswati Sisu Vidya mandira was established to impart free and Gurukul education to children Ms Gayatri a devotee of Guru was associated with the ashram during 2000 to help in spreading and performing spiritual rituals in and aroung the Ashram and looking after the Guru. Guruji as a Paribrajak has led several group of devotees to most of the spiritual places, Dhamas and the Gayatri Tirtha Haridwar. A number of devotees have taken training of Karmakanda, Dhyana, Yoga and other spiritual activity at Haridwar and Mathura. A free Homeopathy medical treatment and medicine service has been provided to the needy people since under the Doctorsip of and his team members. The Ashram construction was expanded and 4 rooms are available at the Ground Floor and Anna Prnya Alaya(Abig Hall and Kitchen)is constructed. Similarly the 1st floor was extended to 4 rooms. Before the Mahaparayan of the Guru in 2015 a new Trusty member was constituted to look after the maintainance of Ashram. With the contribution of Devotees, other organisation and special contribution of Babi and Shridevi the new Gayatri temple is contructed.
